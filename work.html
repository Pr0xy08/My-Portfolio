BEAR PARK BAND

In November of 2022 I begun work along side good friends of mine at the Bear Park Band. 

we begun by bouncing ideas between each other and I asked for personal opinions on both the design and the requirements for the website. 

After retrieving this information I started work on a minimalistic platform which had the capability to promote their work and upcoming gigs. I didn't want said minimalistic philosophy to subtract from the site in anyway. hence why I incorporated a button based navigation system to keep it entertaining. I also incorporated features to buy tickets to their gigs as well as play their music/albums from the site.

Take a look, Or if you want to find out more contact me.

Consett Comedy Festival 

After establishing connections with the Bear Park website I was then given the opportunity to create a website for the yearly (and highly prestigious) comedy festival in Consett.

Beginning work in early December I begun by asking the client for his requirements and personal opinions. 

After settling upon an idea I got to work by firstly creating a home page which would have an "about" section as well as navigation to all the other pages. The other pages would include a "buy tickets" page as well as a "contact" and "line up" page. 

Take a look, Or if you want to find out more contact me.

ProjectDJ Entertainment Services

In early summer of 2023 I begun work on another site, this time for a party and entertainment business. This was very different to anything I had done before and proved to be a good (but also fun) challange.

The client themselves was limited by the amount of assets and ideas in which they could provide me, although this was great for teaching me adaptation and improvisation skills.

I also widened my skillset through this by implementing and creating a form system which would update the client if one of their client requested a booking, this was done directly through the site via booking page!

Take a look, Or if you want to find out more contact me.

Psychic Stu Comedy Website

In late summer of 2023 I started working on another site, this time for a Comedian going by the alias of Psychic Stu. Overall it was a simple task and proved to me how much my skills have developed over the past year.

Specifically the client wanted to be able to share his upcoming events to the site, to make it simple and easier for him to do so I embedded his Facebook posts to the site so that the sites events would be synchronised with his current posts.

Like the previous site I also implemented a third party form system (using Jotforms) so that my client has the ability to receive messages from his clients. 

Take a look, Or if you want to find out more contact me.
